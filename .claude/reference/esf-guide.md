# ESF Quick Reference

## The Five Practices

**1. Position Statement** — Before AI enters, write your stance, what matters most, and what you will not compromise on. This is the gate. Everything downstream depends on it.

**2. Five Questions** — At each decision point, ask: Can I defend this? Is this mine? Did I verify? Would I teach this? Is my disclosure honest? See `templates/five-questions-checklist.md`.

**3. Records of Resistance** — When you reject or revise AI output, document what it suggested, what you chose, and why. See `templates/record-of-resistance.md`.

**4. AI Use Log** — Track tool, prompt, what AI produced, what you kept or changed. See `templates/ai-use-log.md`.

**5. Disclosure** — Attach an honest statement to finished work. Match detail to stakes. See `templates/disclosure-statement.md`.
