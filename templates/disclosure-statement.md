# Disclosure Statement

Attach a version of this to any work produced with AI assistance. Adjust the level of detail to match the stakes and audience.

---

## Short Form (for low-stakes or internal work)

> This [document/design/code] was developed with AI assistance ([tool name]). [Your name] directed all [decisions/arguments/design choices] and verified the final result.

## Standard Form (for professional deliverables)

> **AI Disclosure:** This work was produced through structured human-AI collaboration using [tool name and version]. [Your name] directed all substantive decisions, including [list 2 to 3 key areas: e.g., "the argument structure, source selection, and editorial voice"]. The AI contributed [list specific contributions: e.g., "initial drafting of sections 2 and 4, formatting, and citation formatting"]. All [claims/outputs/code] were verified independently. [Number] Records of Resistance document specific decisions to reject or revise AI suggestions.

## Detailed Form (for high-stakes, published, or academic work)

> **AI Collaboration Disclosure**
>
> **Tool:** [Name, version, provider]
> **Role of AI:** [Specific contributions: drafting, research assistance, code generation, etc.]
> **Role of author:** [What you directed, decided, verified, and created independently]
> **Verification:** [How you checked the work: fact-checking process, testing approach, peer review]
> **Records of Resistance:** [Number] documented instances where AI output was rejected or substantially revised
> **Position Statement:** Written before AI engagement; available on request
>
> The author takes full responsibility for the accuracy and integrity of this work.
